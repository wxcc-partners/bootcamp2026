## How the Rewards Program Works

The Webex Bank Rewards Program follows a simple lifecycle designed to ensure transparency and ease of use.

When you make an eligible purchase, rewards are automatically earned and credited to your account. Over time, these rewards accumulate and become available for redemption through a variety of options, depending on your card type.

Redemption allows you to convert your rewards into tangible value, such as statement credits or travel-related services. If rewards are not used within the defined validity period, they may expire in accordance with program terms.

---

## Earning Rewards

Rewards are earned on most standard purchase transactions, including categories such as dining, retail, groceries, and travel. The rate at which rewards are earned depends on your card type and, in some cases, the category of the purchase.

However, certain transactions are not eligible for rewards. These typically include cash advances, balance transfers, interest charges, fees, and any transactions that are later reversed or refunded.

Webex Bank continuously evaluates spending patterns and may introduce promotional categories or partner offers that allow customers to earn rewards at enhanced rates.

---

## Redemption and Value

Rewards hold no monetary value until they are redeemed. Once eligible, customers may choose from a range of redemption options depending on their card type.

Cashback rewards are typically applied as statement credits, reducing the outstanding balance on the account. Travel rewards may be redeemed for bookings such as flights or hotel stays, often through designated partner platforms.

Rewards are personal to the account holder and cannot be transferred, sold, or exchanged with other individuals.

---

## Expiration and Forfeiture

Rewards are valid for a limited period, which generally ranges between two and five years depending on the card type and program conditions.

If rewards are not redeemed within this timeframe, they will expire automatically. Additionally, rewards may be forfeited under certain circumstances, including account closure, prolonged inactivity, or confirmed fraudulent activity.

Maintaining an active and compliant account is essential to preserving accumulated rewards.

---

## Account Standing and Eligibility

Participation in the rewards program requires that your account remains in good standing at all times. This means that the account must be active, not delinquent, and not subject to collections or suspension.

Webex Bank reserves the right to restrict the earning or redemption of rewards if the account does not meet these conditions.

---

## Card-Specific Conditions

Each card type within the Webex Bank portfolio is subject to specific reward structures and conditions.

The Standard Cashback Card offers a straightforward cashback model with a defined expiration period, typically around three years.

The Travel Rewards Card provides enhanced earning potential for travel-related purchases, with points that may expire between two and four years depending on account activity and usage patterns.

The Platinum Card delivers premium earning rates and extended validity, with rewards typically remaining valid for up to five years. Additional benefits and conditions may apply based on customer eligibility and usage.

---

## Program Limitations and Restrictions

While the Rewards Program is designed to be flexible and rewarding, certain limitations apply.

Rewards cannot be exchanged directly for cash, although cashback may be applied as a statement credit. Purchases that are returned or refunded will result in the reversal of any associated rewards.

Some rewards or promotional benefits may be limited to specific merchants, categories, or time periods, and may not be universally applicable across all transactions.

---

## Security and Fair Use

Webex Bank is committed to maintaining the integrity of its rewards program. Transactions identified as fraudulent, abusive, or in violation of program terms will not qualify for rewards.

In cases of confirmed fraud or misuse, Webex Bank reserves the right to revoke accumulated rewards and take appropriate action on the account.

---

## Changes to the Program

The Rewards & Cashback Program is subject to ongoing review and improvement. Webex Bank reserves the right to modify reward rates, adjust eligibility criteria, update redemption options, or discontinue the program at its discretion.

Where possible, customers will be informed of significant changes in advance.
